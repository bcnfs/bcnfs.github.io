Barcelona Free Software is a creators community of Free Software, also known as Open Source, in Barcelona. Our main activity is a Meetup group, an open gathering for developers and users who share a love for software freedom, but sometimes we organize bigger events too (like the Linux App Summit 2019 or Akademy 2022).

Come to our next meeting to discover new projects and have a drink.

Join our community chat and talk to us and the rest of Free Software enthusiasts in Barcelona.

Subscribe to our mailing list to be informed every time we schedule a meetup.

You can watch the recording of some of our talks on YouTube.

Are you interested in Linux kernel programming? Learn more about our Kernel peer lab.

Anything to say? Please, contact us directly.

We are friends and want to stay friends. Learn about our Code of Conduct.

You can also follow us on Mastodon, Bluesky or LinkedIn.
