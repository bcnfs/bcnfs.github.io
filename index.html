Barcelona Free Software is a creators community of Free Software, also known as Open Source, in Barcelona. Our main activity is a Get Together / Meetup group, an open gathering for developers and users who share a love for software freedom, but sometimes we organize bigger events.

Come to our next meeting to discover new projects and have a drink.

Anything to say? Please, contact us directly.

We are friends and want to stay friends. Learn about our Code of Conduct.

You can also follow us on Mastodon and Twitter.
